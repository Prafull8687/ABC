Virat Kohli

born 5 November 1988) is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the India national team. A right-handed top-order batsman, Kohli is regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. plays for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League (IPL), and has been the team's captain since 2013. October 2017, he has been the top-ranked ODI batsman in the world and is currently 2nd in Test rankings with 886 points.
